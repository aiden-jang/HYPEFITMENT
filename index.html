HYPEFITMENT.



Every tire and rim comes in all kinds of different sizes. Have you ever wondered whether your new tire will fit on your current or new rim, and how your new setup will look compared to your old one? Will it poke out more? Will I gain more ride height? Let's find out with HYPEFITMENT.


HYPEFITMENT visually calculates and compares your current and new setups. You don't need to waste time googling or searching forums for the specs.


Start by entering the specs of your current rim and tire, and then enter your new rim and tire specs. The diagram will be updated right away as well as the blocks showing the overall performance changes. A blue block means your car will perform better in that category.


You can use your mouse or keyboard. Whatever you feel more comfortable with!
